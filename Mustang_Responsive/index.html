Introduction to the 2024 Ford Mustang Mach 1

The 2024 Ford Mustang Mach 1 represents the pinnacle of naturally aspirated 5.0L Mustang performance. As a dream car for many enthusiasts, it delivers an exhilarating combination of power, precision, and heritage.

Performance and Engine

  • 0-60 mph in approximately 4.2 seconds
  • Quarter-mile time: 12.2 seconds
  • Top speed: 168 mph
  • Track-ready suspension with MagneRide® damping

Technology and Features

Equipped with a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster, SYNC 4, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a premium B&O sound system.

Safety

Ford Co-Pilot360™ with driver assistance, blind-spot monitoring, and cross-traffic alerts ensure peace of mind.

Back To The Future

Back To the Future is a film about a teenager Marty McFly, who goes back in time from 1985 to 1955 in a time traveling DeLorean, built by the scientist, Doc Brown. Marty has to make sure his parents fall in love, while racing to return to his time with the help of his new friend doc.
